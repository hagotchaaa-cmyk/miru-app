Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 28, 2026

Your privacy
matters to us.

✦ We never sell your data. We never show ads. Your information is used only to help you find real connections.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Miru, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and password (encrypted)
  • Profile information — your name, age, gender, city, bio, photos and videos you choose to upload
  • Preferences — your intent, vibe, availability, and age range preferences
  • Location — your approximate location (city level) to show nearby matches. We never track your exact GPS coordinates continuously.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app to improve matching quality
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with compatible people using our AI matching algorithm
  • To send you notifications about matches and messages
  • To process your Premium subscription payments
  • To improve our matching algorithm and app experience
  • To ensure safety and prevent abuse

3. What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • We never show you advertisements
  • We never share your exact location with other users — only approximate distance
  • We never share your data with advertisers
  • We never store your payment card details

4. Your Photos and Videos

Photos and videos you upload are stored securely on our servers and are only shown to other Miru users. You can delete your media at any time from your profile settings. When you delete your account, all your media is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing

We only share your data with:

  • Supabase — our secure database and authentication provider
  • Stripe — our payment processor (Premium subscriptions only)
  • Expo — push notification delivery

All third-party providers are contractually required to protect your data.

6. Age Requirement

Miru is only for users 18 years of age or older. By creating an account, you confirm you are at least 18 years old. We will immediately terminate accounts found to belong to minors.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data in transit and at rest. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. We conduct regular security audits.

9. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile and personal data are permanently deleted within 30 days. Some anonymized usage data may be retained for up to 90 days for analytics purposes.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at: privacy@miru.app
