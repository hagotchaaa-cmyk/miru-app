Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026  ·  Operated by Miru
Overview

Miru is a video-first dating app built for real connection. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Miru you agree to the practices described here.

If you have any questions, contact us at contact@miruclub.com and we will respond as quickly as we can.

Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly when you create an account or use the app:

  • Email address — used for account login and communication
  • Name, age, and bio — displayed on your profile
  • Photos and videos — your avatar, posts, and media sent in messages
  • Preferences and interests — entered in your profile or AI Matchmaker settings
  • Messages — sent between you and your matches inside the app
  • Usage data — likes, matches, and interactions within the app

We do not collect precise GPS location. Miru is currently available in the Phoenix, AZ area and uses that as a general location reference only.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Show your profile to other users and surface potential matches
  • Enable real-time messaging between matches
  • Power the AI Matchmaker feature using your stated preferences
  • Process Premium subscription payments securely
  • Send important account and service-related communications
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations
  • Improve and maintain the app

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-party services

Miru uses a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage, and real-time messaging infrastructure
  • Stripe — payment processing for Premium subscriptions. Stripe handles all payment data directly and we never store your card details
  • Google — optional Google sign-in via OAuth 2.0
  • Apple — optional Sign in with Apple via OAuth 2.0
  • Render — web hosting and deployment

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We share only the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. All connections are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you have concerns about the security of your account, contact us immediately at contact@miruclub.com.

Your rights and choices

You have full control over your account and data:

  • Edit your profile name, bio, age, and photos at any time from the Profile tab
  • Pause your account to hide your profile without losing any data
  • Delete your account permanently from the Profile tab — this removes your profile, posts, likes, messages, and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your data or ask questions about what we hold by emailing us

To exercise any of these rights, go to your Profile settings in the app or email contact@miruclub.com.

Children's privacy

Miru is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor is using Miru, please contact us at contact@miruclub.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. For significant changes we will notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of Miru after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to your privacy, we're here to help.
