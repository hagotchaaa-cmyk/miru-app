Common questions
How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap Log in then tap Forgot password? Enter your email and we will send you a reset link.
How do I update my profile photo?
Go to the Profile tab and tap your avatar or the ✦ button on your photo. You can upload a new photo from your library.
Why can't I see my matches?
Matches appear when two users like each other. Keep exploring and liking profiles — matches will show up on your Matches tab.
How do I report someone?
Email us at contact@miruclub.com with the user's name or profile details and we will review and take action promptly.
How do I cancel my Premium subscription?
If you subscribed via our website, email us at contact@miruclub.com and we will cancel it immediately with no questions asked.
Is Miru available outside Phoenix?
Miru is currently focused on the Phoenix, AZ area. We plan to expand to more cities soon.
